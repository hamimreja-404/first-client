Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

We stand by the quality of our training courses. We believe in providing practical, actionable mentorship to help you start your e-commerce journey. To give you peace of mind, we provide a structured refund policy.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee starting from your date of course enrollment. If you feel the course is not a fit for your needs, you can claim a full refund during this period, subject to the eligibility terms outlined below.

2. Refund Eligibility Criteria

To prevent piracy and abuse of our digital learning resources, a refund request will be approved only if:

  • You apply within 7 days of purchase.
  • You have completed/watched less than 20% of the total course video content.
  • You have not downloaded our premium proprietary lists (such as the 800+ Sourced Winning Products Database or Wholesaler Contact Sheets).

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Requests made after 7 days have elapsed from the purchase timestamp.
  • Accounts flagged or suspended due to violation of our Terms of Use (such as account sharing, video recording, or spamming student support groups).
  • Stating lack of time, changing your mind, or choosing not to implement the seller practices taught.

4. How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund claim, please follow these steps:

  1. Email our support team at: support@ecomacademy.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Refund Request - [Your Full Name].
  3. In the email body, provide your registered purchase email address, mobile number, invoice copy, and a brief description of why the course did not meet your expectations.

5. Processing and Payment Timelines

Once your request is received, our audit team will verify your course progression analytics. If eligible, your refund will be approved and initiated within 3-5 business days. The funds will be credited back via your original payment method (UPI, net banking, or credit/debit card) according to standard banking clearing timelines (usually 5-7 business days).
