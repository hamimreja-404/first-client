Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

At Ecom Academy, we stand behind the quality of our practical training. We want to ensure you have a risk-free learning experience, which is why we offer a clear refund policy.

1. 7-Day Guarantee Duration

We offer a hassle-free 7-day money-back guarantee. If you purchase a course and decide it isn't the right fit for you, you can request a full refund within 7 calendar days of your purchase timestamp.

2. Eligibility & Fair Use Criteria

To prevent abuse of our educational resources and protect our proprietary supplier catalogs, refunds are subject to the following fair use conditions:

  • Your account progression must show that you have watched less than 20% of the total course video lectures.
  • You must not have downloaded the spreadsheet calculators or the 800+ winning product supplier database. Once these resources are downloaded, the purchase is non-refundable.
  • Refunds do not apply to live workshops or custom consultation calls that have already been conducted.

3. Refund Request Procedure

To initiate a refund request, please send an email to support@ecomacademy.com from the email address you used during enrollment. Please include:

  • Your Full Name & WhatsApp Phone Number.
  • The name of the course you want a refund for.
  • Your purchase receipt or screenshot.
  • A brief explanation of why the course did not meet your expectations (this feedback helps us improve our syllabus).

4. Processing Timeline

Once we verify your eligibility under the criteria above, we will approve your refund. The funds will be credited back to your original payment method (bank account, UPI, card) within 5 to 7 business days, depending on bank processing speeds.
