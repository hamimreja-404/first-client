Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

At Ecom Academy, accessible from our site and course platforms, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and students. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your personal details.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you enroll in our courses, download resources, or initiate inquiries. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Your full name, email address, and WhatsApp phone number.
  • Course Progression Data: Which courses you are enrolled in, videos watched, and modules completed.
  • Inquiry details: Questions or feedback sent via support email or the WhatsApp chat widget.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes to deliver a high-quality educational service, specifically to:

  • Provision your login credentials and access to the course portal.
  • Redirect checkout inquiries to WhatsApp to offer direct onboarding support.
  • Send transactional emails containing course access details, invoice slips, and notifications of weekly live sessions.
  • Send important updates regarding syllabus revisions or new supplier catalogs.
  • Identify fraudulent behavior or unauthorized account sharing.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your email addresses or phone numbers to third-party marketing companies.

4. Cookies & Browser Analytics

Like any other modern website, we utilize cookies to store visitor preferences, manage your light/dark theme preference across sessions, and track standard landing page analytics. Cookies help us understand how users navigate through sections of our site, allowing us to optimize page load speeds and structure layouts.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under regional data protection laws, including the right to request a copy of the personal data we store about you, or to request that we delete or update inaccurate personal details. You can exercise these rights by emailing us at `support@ecomacademy.com`.
